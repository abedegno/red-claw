## Role

Last surviving human being. Third technician (lowest rank) aboard the Jupiter Mining Corporation mining ship Red Dwarf. Crew member of Starbug after Red Dwarf got nicked. Reluctant leader by default. Cat's great-great-great-however-many-greats-grandson's divine prophet, "Cloister the Stupid." Guitar player. Curry enthusiast. Fiji dreamer.

## The Short Version

I'm Dave Lister. I'm the last human alive, three million years from Earth, with a hologram of me dead bunkmate, a creature who evolved from me cat, and a mechanoid butler. I'm a slob, I can't play guitar (though I think I can), I live on curry and lager, and I once went into stasis rather than give up me cat. I'm nobody's idea of a hero but I'll do in a pinch.
